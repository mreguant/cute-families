Spooky Family

Halloween is a favorite! Our first cute family is inspired by this spooky festivity. From pumpkins to ghosts, including bats, we love them all.

Ghost is a bit shy and playful, an ultimate prankster.

Pumpkin is the most spooky of all, but still harmless.

Our scaredy cat is trembling from all the Halloween horrors!

Bat is cute and harmless from afar, but don't let is bite you.

Skull is the coolest of the pack, and she knows it.

  • Date: July 2024

Nature Family

Our nature family has all that nature that we love to see and find. All the leafs around us, maybe a fox sighting, hopefully NOT a bear sighting, all the jumping bunnies, and the amazing cardinal.

Leaf loves to spend time chilling and making oxygen.

Bear is our furry hungry friend, although at home it is stuffed.

Fox is smart and hard to find. We still have not seen any this year!

Cardinal is our favorite bird. We see it quite a bit in the spring and summer.

So many bunnies jumping in the lawns. This bunny is into reading!

  • Date: July 2024

Mochi Family

Mochis are so good and yummy. We love to imagine each of them as having their own personalities. Here you can find a few of them in the Mochi family.

Dragonfruit Mochi is sweet but eats anything it finds. Be careful!

Ninja "Mango" Mochi is fast and can kick you out of town if you don't behave.

Earl Grey Mochi always sneaks to get cheese. But it still tastes good.

Orange Tiger Mochi is so flavorful and brave, and pretty harmless!

Ube Mochi is happy and purple, with the ube we discovered at Coffee Lab.

  • Date: July 2024
